Obstacle Avoidance — The Lego Test

With two young children and Lego-sized toys on the floor, obstacle avoidance is the single most critical feature. Here's how each model handles it.

Ecovacs X8 Pro Omni

★★★★★

AIVI 3D uses a front-facing camera combined with structured light to identify and avoid objects as small as 5cm. Recognises toys, cords, shoes, and pet waste. Takes photos of obstacles for review in the app. Among the best in class for avoidance.

⚠️ Still may miss very small items (cat-toy size)

Eufy S1 Pro

★★★★

Camera-based AI with obstacle recognition. Reddit users report excellent cord avoidance and decent toy avoidance. Strong for larger obstacles but less refined than the X8 or Curv for very small items. The Eufy X10 Pro Omni variant is specifically praised for cord avoidance.

⚠️ Camera-only system — struggles more in low light

Roborock Qrevo Curv

★★★★★

Reactive AI with camera, structured light, and cross-projection. The unique self-lifting chassis physically climbs over some obstacles rather than just avoiding them — particularly effective with cords and medium toys. Industry-leading edge case handling.

⚠️ Still cannot identify/avoid items under ~5cm reliably

Honest advice: No robot vacuum can reliably avoid Lego bricks or very small toys. You'll still want to do a quick floor tidy before scheduled runs. These models are the best available at trying.

Which One Should You Choose?

Choose the Ecovacs X8 Pro Omni if…

  • ✅ You want the best all-rounder as rated by PCMag
  • ✅ Self-cleaning roller mop mid-run matters (no spreading dirt)
  • ✅ You want the best value at current EOFY pricing (50% off)
  • ✅ Mix of carpet and hard floors with high mop lift (15mm)
  • ✅ You prioritise strong AI obstacle avoidance

Choose the Eufy S1 Pro if…

  • ✅ Mopping is your #1 priority — the HydroJet roller is best-in-class
  • ✅ Budget-conscious — lowest price at $999
  • ✅ Brand familiarity (Amy's dad already owns and likes Eufy)
  • ✅ Mostly hard floors with some carpet
  • ✅ You value the Eufy app experience and ecosystem

Choose the Roborock Qrevo Curv if…

  • ✅ You have thresholds or multi-level floors — lifting chassis is unique
  • ✅ Edge and corner cleaning matters (extendable arm is the best tested)
  • ✅ You want the highest suction (18,500 Pa)
  • ✅ Premium features justify the extra $250 over the X8
  • ✅ You like the curved design and unique aesthetic
