The Lego Test

Obstacle avoidance, ranked

With young kids and small toys on the floor, this is the feature that matters most day to day.

AIVI 3D
★★★★★

Camera + structured light. Identifies and avoids objects down to ~5cm. Recognises toys, cords, shoes, and pet waste. Photos of obstacles available in-app for review.

Still misses very small items (cat-toy size).
Camera AI
★★★★

Camera-based detection. Reddit users report excellent cord avoidance and solid toy avoidance. Strong for larger obstacles, less refined for very small items.

Camera-only — struggles in low light. Less precise than structured-light systems.
Reactive AI
★★★★★

Camera + structured light + cross-projection. The unique lifting chassis can climb over some obstacles rather than just avoiding them. Industry-leading edge case handling.

Cannot reliably identify items under ~5cm.
Honest advice: No robot vacuum can reliably dodge every Lego brick. A quick floor tidy before a scheduled run is still the best defence. These three are the best available at trying.
Decision Guide

Which one is yours?

Pick the Ecovacs X8 Pro Omni if…

  • You want the strongest all-rounder as rated by PCMag Australia
  • A self-cleaning roller mop mid-run matters (no spreading dirty water)
  • You want the best value at current EOFY pricing — 50% off RRP
  • You have a mix of carpet and hard floors (highest mop lift at 15mm)
  • Strong AI obstacle avoidance is non-negotiable

Pick the Eufy S1 Pro if…

  • Mopping performance is your #1 priority — HydroJet is best-in-class
  • Budget matters — lowest price at $999
  • Brand familiarity counts (Amy's dad already owns and likes Eufy)
  • Your home is mostly hard floors with some carpet
  • You value the Eufy app ecosystem and experience

Pick the Roborock Qrevo Curv if…

  • You have thresholds or multi-level floors — the lifting chassis is unique
  • Edge and corner cleaning matters (extendable arm is best tested)
  • You want the highest suction available (18,500 Pa)
  • Premium navigation features justify the $250 premium over the X8
  • You love the curved aesthetic and Roborock build quality
